Non-Surgical Fat Reduction Treatments

Non-surgical fat reduction offers a safe, effective way to contour your body without the need for surgery or downtime. 

Cryolipolysis, radiofrequency skin tightening, and 3D dermology treatments target stubborn fat, improve skin elasticity, and enhance body confidence. 

Designed for those seeking visible results with minimal disruption to daily life, non-surgical fat reduction helps sculpt and refine your shape for a more toned and contoured appearance.

Body Aesthetic Courses

Cryolipolysis (Fat Freezing)

A non-invasive fat reduction treatment that freezes and eliminates stubborn fat cells, contouring the body without surgery.

How It Works: Controlled cooling targets fat cells, triggering their natural breakdown and removal.

Duration: 45–60 minutes per area

Pre-Treatment: Stay hydrated and avoid anti-inflammatory medications 24 hours prior.

Post-Treatment: Mild redness and numbness may occur; results develop over several weeks.

Frequency: 1 session per area every 6–12 weeks

Interim Rest Period: Minimum 6 weeks between sessions on the same area (to allow the body to naturally process and eliminate fat cells)




RF Skin Tightening

A skin-firming treatment that uses radiofrequency energy to stimulate collagen production and improve skin elasticity.

How It Works: Heat penetrates deep into the skin, tightening existing collagen and encouraging new collagen growth.

Duration: 30–45 minutes

Pre-Treatment: Avoid sun exposure and retinol products for 48 hours before treatment.

Post-Treatment: Skin may appear slightly red; hydration and SPF use are recommended.

Frequency: 1 session per week

Interim Rest Period: 7 days between treatments

Recommended Course: Typically 6–8 sessions for optimal results




Dermology

A non-invasive treatment combining vacuum suction and rollers to reduce cellulite and improve skin texture by enhancing lymphatic drainage.

How It Works: The treatment utilizes a combination of vacuum suction and mechanical rollers to stimulate blood flow and lymphatic drainage, effectively reducing the appearance of cellulite and promoting firmer skin.

Duration: Approximately 30–60 minutes, depending on the treatment area.

Frequency: 1–2 sessions per week

Interim Rest Period: Minimum 3–4 days between sessions to allow lymphatic drainage and skin recovery.

Recommended Course: Typically 6–8 sessions for optimal cellulite reduction and skin tightening.

Pre-Treatment: Maintain adequate hydration and avoid applying heavy lotions or oils to the treatment area on the day of the session.

Post-Treatment: Mild redness or bruising may occur; it is advisable to continue hydrating and engage in light physical activity to support lymphatic drainage.
