Cryolipolysis (Fat Freezing)

A non-invasive fat reduction treatment that freezes and eliminates stubborn fat cells, contouring the body without surgery.

How It Works: Controlled cooling targets fat cells, triggering their natural breakdown and removal.

Duration: 45–60 minutes per area

Frequency: 1 session per area every 6–12 weeks

Interim Rest Period: Minimum 6 weeks between sessions on the same area

Pre-Treatment: Stay hydrated and avoid anti-inflammatory medications 24 hours prior.

Post-Treatment: Mild redness and numbness may occur; results develop over several weeks.

RF Skin Tightening

A skin-firming treatment that uses radiofrequency energy to stimulate collagen production and improve skin elasticity.

How It Works: Heat penetrates deep into the skin, tightening existing collagen and encouraging new collagen growth.

Duration: 30–45 minutes

Frequency: 1 session per week

Interim Rest Period: 7 days between treatments

Pre-Treatment: Avoid sun exposure and retinol products for 48 hours before treatment.

Post-Treatment: Skin may appear slightly red; hydration and SPF use are recommended.

Dermaroller (Microneedling)

A rejuvenating skin treatment that promotes collagen production, reducing scars, fine lines, and improving overall skin texture.

How It Works: Tiny needles create micro-injuries in the skin, triggering a natural healing response.

Duration: 45–60 minutes

Frequency: Every 4–6 weeks

Interim Rest Period: Minimum 4 weeks between sessions

Pre-Treatment: Avoid retinoids and exfoliants for 48 hours before treatment.

Post-Treatment: Skin may be red and sensitive for 24–48 hours; apply soothing serums and SPF.

Optimal Treatment Combinations

Cryolipolysis (Fat Freezing)

Frequency: 1 session per area every 6–12 weeks

Interim Rest Period: Minimum 6 weeks between sessions on the same area

Can Be Combined With:

  • ✅ RF Skin Tightening (on different areas immediately, or after 2 weeks in the same area)
  • ✅ Dermaroller (Microneedling) (if done on a different area, or after 4 weeks in the same area)

Cannot Be Combined With:

  • ❌ Additional Cryolipolysis in the same area (requires a 6-week gap)
  • ❌ RF Skin Tightening in the same treated area (must wait 2 weeks)
  • ❌ Dermaroller in the same body treatment area (must wait 4 weeks)

RF Skin Tightening

Frequency: 1 session per week

Interim Rest Period: 7 days between treatments

Recommended Course: Typically 6–8 sessions for optimal results

Can Be Combined With:

  • ✅ Cryolipolysis (on different areas immediately, or after 2 weeks in the same area)
  • ✅ Dermaroller (Microneedling) (on different areas immediately, or after 1 week in the same area)

Cannot Be Combined With:

  • ❌ Cryolipolysis in the same area on the same day (must wait 2 weeks)
  • ❌ Dermaroller in the same area on the same day (must wait 1 week)

Dermaroller (Microneedling)

Frequency: Every 4–6 weeks

Interim Rest Period: Minimum 4 weeks between sessions

Recommended Course: 3–6 sessions depending on skin concerns

Can Be Combined With:

  • ✅ Cryolipolysis (on different areas immediately, or after 4 weeks in the same area)
  • ✅ RF Skin Tightening (on different areas immediately, or after 1 week in the same area)

Cannot Be Combined With:

  • ❌ RF Skin Tightening in the same area on the same day (must wait 1 week)
  • ❌ Cryolipolysis in the same area on the same day (must wait 4 weeks)
