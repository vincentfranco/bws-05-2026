Reverse the signs of aging and restore youthful hands with CACI Hand Rejuvenation. This advanced, non-invasive treatment improves skin texture, reduces pigmentation, and delivers deep hydration for smoother, firmer hands.

Restore, Replenish, Rejuvenate

This treatment uses microcurrent technology to stimulate skin renewal while a collagen-rich mask infuses intense moisture into the skin. The result? Hydrated, firmer, and more youthful-looking hands.

Treatment Duration

The CACI Hand Rejuvenation treatment lasts approximately 30 minutes and is completely pain-free, making it an easy addition to your skincare routine.

  • Experience dryness, wrinkles, or pigmentation on their hands.

Standard Pre-Care Advice

To ensure optimal results, follow these guidelines before your treatment:

  • Arrive with clean, product-free hands (no lotions or oils).
  • Stay hydrated before your appointment for optimal results.
  • If you have any skin conditions or allergies, inform your therapist beforehand.

Standard Post-Care Guidelines

To maintain and enhance your results, follow these post-treatment recommendations:

  • Avoid direct sun exposure for at least 24 hours.
  • Apply a hydrating hand cream regularly to maintain results.
  • Drink plenty of water to support collagen production and skin hydration.
